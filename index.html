Broadcast without boundaries

One stream for multiple resolutions

With StreamKit Cloud, you can easily broadcast from many locations. You don't need a high-end wired connection to stream and can use your 3G/4G cellular modem connection.

Our technology allow you to send one HD quality and have the StreamKit Cloud adjust the quality to lower resolutions so that your viewers have the best adapting streaming experience.
StreamKit Cloud is compatible with common industry's hardware and software encoders including Teradek Vidiu, Digital Rapids ( Imagine Communications ), Wirecast, FMLE and others.

Native Apps

Your own TV and Mobile App

Go beyond web streaming and keep your audience closer by giving them access to your videos through native applications.

StreamKit enables you to create your own personalized application, with your own branding. Native applications make your live stream as well as your recorded videos easier to access from Android, iOS devices or TVs. TV Apps offered through Roku and Chromecast devices bring your content on the big TV screen.

StreamKit keeps your applications up-to-date so that you don't need to worry about maintaining updates and compatibilities.

Analytics

Knowing your audience is the key to success

With StreamKit you have complete insight into your viewers real-time engagement with information such as time spent on video, device type, and bandwidth used with all beautifully organized and displayed in a user friendly dashboard.
